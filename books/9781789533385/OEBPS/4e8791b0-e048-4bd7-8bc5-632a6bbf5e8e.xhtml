Formatting the Story

Although the content of the Story is essential, the importance of visual aspects of the Story's presentation should never be underestimated. If the visual layout of the presentation is sloppy, it can leave a bad impression on the audience and take away the credibility of the content of the Story. We certainly wouldn't want our hard work to be ignored due to bad formatting. So, let's pick up some tricks that can improve the visual identity of our presentation.
