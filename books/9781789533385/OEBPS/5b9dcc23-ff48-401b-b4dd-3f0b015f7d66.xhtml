How to do it…

  1. Right-click on the Year field under Dimensions.
  2. In the drop-down menu, navigate to Create | Set… as shown in the following screenshot:

  1. In the Name field, change the name of the set from Set 1 to Above average employment years:

  1. Click on the Condition tab and select By field.
  2. From the first drop-down menu, choose Employment.
  3. From the second drop-down menu, select Average.
  4. From the third drop-down menu, select the >= (greater or equal to) sign.
  5. In the number field, type 297027. This is the overall average of employment for all years.
  1. Click OK to exit the window:

  1. A new section, Sets, has appeared in the Data pane, below Measures. It contains our new set, denoted by the set symbol . Drag and drop the set we created onto Color in the Marks label.
  2. Drag and drop Year from Dimensions into the Columns shelf.
  3. Right-click on the Year pill in the Columns shelf, and in the drop-down menu switch to Discrete.
  4. Drag and drop Employment from Measures into the Rows shelf.
  5. Hover over the Employment pill in the Rows shelf and click on the white arrow that appears on it.
  1. In the drop-down menu, navigate to Measure (Sum) and select Average. This will provide the following output:
