How it works...

In this recipe, we have created a basic dashboard. First, we have created a couple of worksheets. Then, we have placed them all in one dashboard.

Dashboards can hold multiple worksheets. Apart from worksheets, they can also contain images, links, text boxes, and web pages. Although dashboards can contain many elements, they are meant to present data in an easy-to-read manner, so you should always strive to maintain a clean look and not make your dashboard overcrowded.
