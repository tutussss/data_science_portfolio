How to do it...

To follow along, open the Marimekko packaged workbook: Mekko.twbx and xAPI-Edu-Data.csv. We are going to compare gender, parent satisfaction with school, and student participation by raised hands.

It is recommended you start with a text table to get the calculations correct.

  1. Double-click on Measure Values:

  1. Add the Raised Hands to the Measure Values card:

  1. Add Raised Hands per Column to the Measure Values card:
