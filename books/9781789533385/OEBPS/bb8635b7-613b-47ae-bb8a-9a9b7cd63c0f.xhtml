Analyzing the results by age groups reveals some interesting differences in the level of awareness. Almost 75% of people aged 45 or older know that milk cartons can be recycled in Serbia. On the other hand, more than 60% buyers in the 15-24 age group do not know about this possibility. Based on that, we can conclude that the 15-24 group is the least informed and that we need to make a concerted effort to raise their awareness. That is our first clue. Let's investigate some more.
