How to do it…

  1. We start off connected to two data sources: Internet_satisfaction.csv and Internet_usage.csv and a new open blank worksheet opened.
  2. In the main menu toolbar, navigate to Data.

  1. From the drop-down menu, select Edit Relationships…:

  1. In the Relationships window, select Custom.
  2. Click on Add…:

  1. In the Add/Edit Field Mapping window, click on Area in one pane and Region in the other pane, so that they're highlighted in blue:

  1. Click on OK.

  1. Mapping of Region and Area will appear in the Relationships window now. Click on OK to exit it as well:

We've successfully blended our data sources!
