Introduction

So far, you have learned how to create individual charts and dashboards. In this chapter, you will go a step further and learn how to connect them in a meaningful Story. The Tableau Story functionality enables you to order visualizations in a logical and simple manner that helps your audience understand your insights better. Data that is presented within a Story is much easier to remember. Also, telling data stories gives you an opportunity to present your results in a broader context. Making stories in a Tableau is a lot like playing a game of "connect the dots". You just need to make the right links between your points and a picture will appear.
