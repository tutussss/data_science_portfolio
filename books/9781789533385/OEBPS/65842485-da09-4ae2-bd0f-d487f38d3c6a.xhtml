How it works...

Writing effective headlines is a skill that requires some practice. Luckily, there are some tips that we can use to make them better.

When writing headlines, we must make sure that they meet the following requirements:

  • They clearly communicate the most important finding
  • They are not data chart titles
  • They do not repeat the figures from the charts
  • They should use a verb
  • They should use transitional words (such as but, moreover, and in addition to)
  • They are no longer than five lines
