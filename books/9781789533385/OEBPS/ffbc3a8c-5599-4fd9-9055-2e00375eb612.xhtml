Data blending

Sometimes, our data sources might not be suitable for joining for multiple reasons. For example, they might be on different levels of aggregation, resulting in duplicates upon joining, or you might wish to use data source types that don't support cross-database joins, such as Google Analytics. But, we also want to make visualizations that contain fields from different data sources.
