Setting and formatting dashboard title

  1. In the Objects pane, check the box in front of Show dashboard title:

  1. Double-click on the title and, in the Edit Title window, change it from Dashboard 1 to Satisfaction. Select the title text and change the font size to 24, change the color to orange, and apply bold font:

  1. Alternatively, navigate to the main menu toolbar, click on Dashboard, and select Format.
  1. The Format Dashboard pane will appear on the left-hand side. Under Dashboard Title, you can adjust the text, font, color, and size under Font; text alignment under Alignment; text background color under Shading; and border of the title under Border:
