Besides choosing the top N members of a dimension, Tableau also allows us to choose the bottom N. In the Filter [Winery] window, click on the Top drop-down menu and select Bottom, as shown in the following screenshot:

It is also possible to filter the top/bottom N members by custom formula. In the Filter [Winery] window, select By formula, and Tableau will allow you to type in your custom expression to filter by.
