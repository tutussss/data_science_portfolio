Formatting worksheet titles

  1. From the main menu toolbar, navigate to Dashboard and select Format.
  2. Under Worksheet Titles, click on the Font drop-down arrow, change the text color to orange, and apply bold font.
  3. Click on Shading to change the background color to light gray, and move the slider under the palette to the left to make the shade even lighter:

  1. Alternatively, you can format the titles individually by double-clicking on them, selecting the title text in the Edit Title window, and adjusting font size, color, alignment, and so on.
